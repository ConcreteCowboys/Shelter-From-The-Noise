# =========================================

SHELTER FROM THE NOISE
A New Rock Musical by Tommy B.
Based on a true story.

This is an interactive multimedia presentation featuring:

* 14 text scenes

* 16 images (click to enlarge) plus 4 images of the Real Band 

* 15 embedded songs

* Full script available as a downloadable PDF

All content runs locally — no internet connection required.

---

## HOW TO START

1. Locate and open the file **start.html**.

   * This opens the splash page with a glowing **START PRESENTATION** button.
   * Click that button to launch the full multimedia presentation (index.html).
2. Alternatively, you can open **index.html** directly to skip the intro screen.

---

## NAVIGATION & INTERACTION

* **Scene Layout:**
  Each of the 14 scenes is presented in a two-column format:
  • Left side: Scene image
  • Right side: Scene text and one or two audio tracks

* **Playing Songs:**
  Each scene contains its own embedded song(s).
  • Click the play button on any audio player to hear that scene’s track.
  • Scene 11 features *two songs* (labeled A and B).

* **Full-Screen Images:**
  • Click any scene image to view it full-screen.
  • Click anywhere on the enlarged image to return to the normal view.

* **Download the Full Script:**
  • At the end of Scene 14, click the **Download the Full Script** button.
  • This opens or saves the PDF file:
  `assets/Shelter_from_the_Noise_Script.pdf

* **The Real Band
  • This opens 4 images of the real band this story is based on

## TIPS

• For best performance, open in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.
• Make sure all folders (audio, images, assets) stay in the same directory as index.html.
• Audio may not autoplay depending on browser settings — click play manually.
